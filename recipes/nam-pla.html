Nam Pla

Description

Nam pla is a traditional thai condiment, usually consisting of fish sauce, rice vinegar, thai chilis and some sugar. You can use to spice up your stir fry, fried rice, curry or really anything you'd like to add that quintessential thai kick to.

Ingredients

  • Fish Sauce
  • Thai Chilis
  • Rice Vinegaar
  • Sugar
Instructions
  1. First assemble the ingredients and a find a pint mason jar
  2. Next, chop 3-4 chilis into very thin circles (more or less depending on how spicy you want it)
  3. Fill the contents of the mason jar with 1/2 rice vinegar and 1/2 fish sauce
  4. Add the chilis and 1-2 tablespoons of sugar
  5. It technically doesn't need refrigeration, and can be used immediately although it's best after sitting for atleast 24 hours
